# Roberto v22 Roadmap

## Objective
Calibrate Roberto to personal doctrine and feedback signals.

## Scope

### 1) Doctrine Assets
- `profile/doctrine.md`
- `profile/tags.yaml`
- `profile init` creates templates.
- `profile show` prints active doctrine/taxonomy.

### 2) Feedback Loop
- Command: `feedback mark --card <id> --type good|bad|wrong_pile|wrong_story`.
- Store feedback in SQLite table `card_feedback`.
- Feedback score shifts winnow ranking in Greene cycle.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Doctrine files are first-class and versionable.
- Feedback modifies downstream ranking behavior.
- Calibration remains deterministic and auditable.
