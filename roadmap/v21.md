# Roberto v21 Roadmap

## Objective
Generate chapter structure + argumentation + research gaps from keeper cards.

## Scope

### 1) Chapter Emergence Engine
- Propose chapter candidates in three TOC styles:
  - thematic
  - chronological
  - strategy
- Each chapter stores supporting card IDs and source refs.
- Output note: `notes/greene/chapters/<YYYY-MM-DD>.md`.

### 2) Argumentation Layer
- Build:
  - strongest argument
  - strongest counterargument
  - what would change my mind
  - watch next
  - assumption register
- Output note: `notes/greene/argumentation/<YYYY-MM-DD>.md`.

### 3) Gap Finder
- Flag weak evidence depth and missing mechanism.
- Output note: `notes/greene/gaps/<YYYY-MM-DD>.md`.

### 4) Storage
- SQLite table: `chapter_candidates`.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Chapter proposals are citation-backed and editable.
- Argument/counter are explicit, not blended.
- Gap list yields concrete follow-up questions.
